>Simple Lemon Pepper Salmon for One

Details

Serves: 1

Number of ingredients: 4

To prep: 5 minutes

To cook: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Nutrition per serving

Energy: 1920kJ

Total fat: 26g

Saturated fat: 5g

Carbohydrate: 0.5g

Fibre: 0.6g

Sodium: 61.2mg

{% comment %}

Exchanges: 2

{% endcomment %}

Protein:34g

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon fillet, skin of
  • Olive oil spray
  • ½ lemon 
  • Cracked pepper 

Method

  1. Prepare a small baking dish with alfoil, using enough foil to create a pocket around the fish. 
  2. Lightly spray foil and salmon with olive oil.
  3. Cut a few slices of lemon, then juice the remainder.
  4. Cover with foil, creating a pocket for the salmon to cook. 
  5. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes for medium to well done
  6. Remove from foil and discard lemon slices.  
  7. Serve with mashed low GI potato mash (or try cauliflower mash) and steamed vegetables.
